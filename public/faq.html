Refresheet help and service basics

Answers for new users who want to understand the app, Todaki, score structure, login, rankings, and data behavior.

service FAQoffice refreshspreadsheet-inspired games

What is Refresheet?

Refresheet is a spreadsheet-inspired web app for short office refresh games, quick focus resets, and lightweight companion interaction.

What is Todaki?

Todaki is the digital pet companion inside Refresheet. It reacts to care actions, short sessions, and growth feedback.

How do scores and performance records work?

Game sessions can produce score or performance records. The app uses those records to show personal progress and ranking context where supported.

What is the employee name system?

The employee name is a display name used inside the office-themed interface. It helps rankings and comments feel consistent without exposing unnecessary personal details.

Is login required?

You can view the app interface without logging in, but saved scores, profile settings, comments, and ranking participation may require login.

Does Refresheet store data?

Refresheet stores account, profile, score, ranking, and service interaction data needed to provide app features. Legal and privacy details are provided through the service terms and privacy policy.

How are rankings updated?

Rankings reflect supported game results after a valid session is saved. Daily, weekly, or monthly views may be shown depending on the game and page context.

Does Refresheet support mobile?

Yes. Refresheet is responsive and can be used on mobile browsers, though the spreadsheet-inspired interface is most spacious on larger screens.
