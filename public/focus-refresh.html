Short sessions by design

Refresheet games are built around quick gameplay and lightweight interaction so a refresh can stay brief.

short sessionquick gameplaymicro refresh

The best refresh loop for a busy day is one that respects time. Refresheet aims for sessions that can be understood quickly, played briefly, and closed without a long cooldown.

Why 1 to 3 minutes

A short session is long enough to create a shift in attention, but short enough to keep the user's main work context intact. This is why the games use simple controls, visible progress, and clear end states.

Light loop

Small decisions and compact boards make the interaction easy to leave.

Minimal interruption

The design supports recovery without pulling attention into a long session.

Refresheet keeps its games short so refresh moments remain useful during the workday.
