Micro-break Guide

A short mental reset for the workday

A micro-break is a small pause that helps attention recover without turning into a long interruption.

micro-breakfocus refreshoffice refresh

Long work blocks can make even simple decisions feel heavier. A short mental reset creates a boundary: pause, interact with something lightweight, then return with a cleaner sense of attention.

Why short breaks can help

Refresheet treats the break as a compact loop. The interaction should be easy to understand, quick to finish, and visually calm. That is why the games avoid heavy onboarding and keep the workspace context visible.

1 minute

Check a simple score, feed Todaki, or make a few moves.

3 minutes

Complete a short session and return before the break becomes a new task.

A refresh, not a detour

The purpose is not to replace work with play. It is to give the mind a small change of pace, then make it easy to return.

Refresheet supports lightweight micro-break routines through short, familiar interactions.
